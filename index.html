AccumTech

AccumTech’s mission is to power connectivity for health insurance carriers so they can focus on delivering modern solutions to their customers. Today’s members and employers all demand interconnected health plans with a full complement of offerings. These interconnected plans need coordination among all of the service providers filling the major health components like medical and pharmacy or ancillary benefits like dental and a variety of other plan features. AccumTech’s solutions allow these providers to connect their data and communicate member systems.

The ClaimGen system smoothly connects all of these systems together by creating a central hub for managing the data. Each organization becomes its node and connects to the hub using native formats & data rules.

AccumTech’s Enrollment Engine allows employers to connect their human resources systems directly using the native formats and layouts of the system. AccumTech will create custom couplings for those systems and draw it into the Enrollment Hub. From there, the data can be directed to all product providers and the Enrollment Hub will compare the employer rosters against the carriers to find discrepancies – before it results in waste or member abrasion.

SmartAisle

SmartAisle is the World's first voice-powered retail shopping assistant. It is an innovative, new technology platform designed specifically to revolutionize the customer experience in brick & mortar retail stores. Through a combination of an intuitive Voice interface, connected LED lights affixed to the shelf and a powerful artificial intelligence working behind the scenes, it provides expert product recommendations, education and support to shoppers at the all-important point of decision. As Software Product Manager, I am responsible for developing and maintaining SmartAisle's applications, backend systems and APIs. Throughout our development process, I manage a team of engineers, overseeing our design, build, QA and release phases. Working on a product that connects hardware and software has been a new, challenging experience for me and it has taught me a lot about task dependency and sprint management (epsically for interconnected features).

Marilyn

Marilyn is a predictive commerce application, featuring nine interconnected technologies, designed to increase stakeholder confidence at every critical decision point across marketing. Our users are able to track custom datapoints across thousands of historical marketing programs to analize their budgeting, spend and results. Utilizing this data, we are able to deliver live reporting/results and forcast future performance. As Software Product Manager, I am responsible managing a team of engineers, overseeing our design, build, QA and release phases. In my experience, the most common product management challenge has been navigating the conflicting and ever-changing demands of key stakeholders. I have enjoyed overcoming this challenge by defining MVPs that meet the end user's basic needs and by building out product roadmaps that prioritize the most practical features.
